About Us

We make beautiful dresses for strong women. At Medinat Keshiro we believe every woman deserves the best in what they wear, and every woman is a queen who deserves affordable high-quality clothes. We specialize in excellent and creative designs with perfect completion to your needs. We craft outfits tailored to each individual, blending American styles with rich African styles.
